Adoption, A Smarter Option

Your local pet store is almost always getting their puppies from a "backyard" breeder, or a puppy mill. The majority of purebred dogs are the product of irresponsible backyard breeders. How can you stop puppy mills? There is only one way. Take away their profits. Remember before you buy a puppy, keep in mind that adoption is the most humane option.
